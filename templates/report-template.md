# Report Template

Standard structure for all skill report outputs. Every report file generated
by any skill MUST follow this structure.

## Template

```markdown
# [Report Title]

**URL/Subject:** [what was analyzed]
**Date:** [YYYY-MM-DD]
**Skill:** [skill-name]
**Overall Score:** [X/100] ([Grade/Interpretation])

---

## Executive Summary

[2-3 sentences: what was found, overall health, single most impactful fix]

## Quick Wins (Do This Week)

1. **[Action]** — [Expected impact]
2. **[Action]** — [Expected impact]
3. **[Action]** — [Expected impact]

---

## Full Analysis

### [Dimension 1]
**Score:** X/Y
[Details, evidence, specific findings]

### [Dimension 2]
**Score:** X/Y
[Details, evidence, specific findings]

---

## Recommendations (Prioritized)

| Priority | Action | Impact | Effort | Expected Result |
|----------|--------|--------|--------|-----------------|
| 1 | [action] | HIGH | LOW | [result] |
| 2 | [action] | HIGH | MEDIUM | [result] |
| 3 | [action] | MEDIUM | LOW | [result] |

---

## Next Steps

- [ ] [First action item]
- [ ] [Second action item]
- [ ] [Third action item]

---

*Generated by SaaS Growth Marketing Skills — [skill-name]*
*github.com/ekinciio/saas-growth-marketing-skills*
```

## Usage

All skills reference this template when generating reports. The structure
ensures consistency across all 15 skills while allowing each skill to
customize the "Full Analysis" section for its specific dimensions.
