## Recommended Onboarding Pattern: Progressive Disclosure

**Why this pattern fits Notion:**

Notion's core challenge is that the product can do *everything* — but new users don't need everything on day 1. Progressive disclosure solves this by:

1. **Day 1:** Show only the essentials (pages, basic blocks, one template)
2. **Week 1:** Introduce databases, relations, and team collaboration
3. **Week 2-4:** Reveal power features (formulas, rollups, API, AI agents)

This pattern works because:
- Reduces cognitive overload for new users
- Matches the learning curve to the user's growing expertise
- Prevents "Notion Paralysis" by limiting initial choices
- Preserves the depth that power users love
