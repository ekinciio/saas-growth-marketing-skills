# Case Study: Full Growth Audit of Notion.com

> 12 of 18 possible reports generated by running 10 skills + 2 agents on Notion.com
> Analysis date: March 2026

## About This Case Study

We ran a selection of skills and agents from this toolkit against [Notion.com](https://www.notion.com) to demonstrate what a full SaaS growth audit looks like. The remaining 6 reports (subscription-metrics, pricing-analyzer, plg-funnel-analyzer, retention-playbook, local-seo-optimizer, metrics-analyst) require internal business data and are not included in this public demo.

Notion is a $10B+ productivity platform with 100M+ users - one of the most successful SaaS companies in the world. Even at this scale, our skills found actionable insights and areas for improvement.

**This is not a criticism of Notion.** It's a demonstration of what these skills can uncover for any SaaS product - from early-stage startups to billion-dollar companies.

## Disclaimer

- This is a point-in-time analysis for demonstration purposes only
- Scores and findings reflect the state of the website at the time of analysis and may change as websites are updated
- Metric-based reports use publicly available estimates, not internal company data
- This analysis is based entirely on publicly accessible information (HTML, meta tags, structured data, public APIs)

## Key Takeaways

1. **GEO Score 68/100** — Strong brand authority (92/100) but zero structured data and no llms.txt file hold back AI search visibility
2. **CRO Score 74/100** — Excellent social proof (10/10) and hero clarity, but missing FAQ, trust badges, and urgency mechanics
3. **ASO Score 72/100** — Great ratings (4.78 stars, 79K reviews) but zero iPhone screenshots and generic release notes
4. **Onboarding Score 80/100 (B+)** — Best-in-class signup friction (22/100) but weak post-signup guidance ("Notion Paralysis")
5. **Brand Authority 44/50** — 61K+ mentions across HN, GitHub, Reddit; "Notion alternative" is a defined product category
6. **PLG Score 42/50** — Best-in-class product-led growth with collaborative and template viral loops
7. **Review Sentiment 4.2/5.0** — Gap between professional platforms (G2: 4.7) and consumer platforms (Trustpilot: ~3.0)
