# Xquik Social Intel Scoring

Use this rubric to turn X data into a growth marketing signal.

The bundled script calculates a 0-10 lexical pre-score from purchase-intent and
pain terms. Apply the remaining factors manually after reviewing the SaaS
category, post date, and engagement metrics.

| Factor | Score | Guidance |
|--------|-------|----------|
| Purchase intent | 0-5 | Mentions buying, evaluating, switching, cancelling, or asking for tools |
| Pain intensity | 0-5 | Clear problem, urgency, workaround, or frustration |
| ICP fit | 0-5 | Matches the SaaS category, buyer, role, or use case |
| Recency | 0-3 | Recent posts score higher than stale posts |
| Engagement | 0-3 | Replies, reposts, likes, or discussion depth |

Priority score = purchase intent + pain intensity + ICP fit + recency + engagement.

Treat the score as a ranking aid, not a factual claim. Always quote or summarize the evidence that drove the score.
