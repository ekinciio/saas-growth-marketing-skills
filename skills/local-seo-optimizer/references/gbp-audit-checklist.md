# Google Business Profile Audit Checklist

A comprehensive checklist of 35+ items to evaluate GBP profile completeness. Each item is scored as complete, partial, or missing.

| # | Item | Weight | Notes |
|---|------|--------|-------|
| 1 | Business name (matches real-world name) | High | Must match signage and legal name exactly |
| 2 | Primary category selected | High | Choose the most specific category available |
| 3 | Secondary categories added (up to 9) | Medium | Add all relevant secondary categories |
| 4 | Street address verified | High | Must be a real, physical location |
| 5 | Service area defined (if applicable) | Medium | For businesses that serve customers at their location |

## Scoring Guide

### Per-Item Scoring
- **Complete (2 points):** Item fully addressed, optimized
- **Partial (1 point):** Item exists but needs improvement
- **Missing (0 points):** Item not addressed at all

### Category Weights
- Basic Information: 25%
- Business Description: 10%
- Photos and Visual Content: 20%
- Services/Products/Menu: 10%
- Attributes and Highlights: 10%
- Google Posts: 10%
- Reviews and Q&A: 15%

### Score Ranges
- **90-100:** Excellent - fully optimized profile, maintain current efforts
- **70-89:** Good - minor gaps, address missing items for quick wins
- **50-69:** Fair - several gaps impacting visibility, prioritize high-weight items
- **30-49:** Needs improvement - significant items missing, start with basics
- **Below 30:** Critical - profile is severely incomplete, immediate action needed

## Quick Win Priorities

When a profile scores below 70, focus on these high-impact items first:

1. Complete all basic information fields (items 1-9)
2. Write a full 750-character description with keywords (items 10-13)
3. Upload logo and cover photo (items 15-16)
4. Add at least 10 photos across categories (items 17-21)
5. List all services with descriptions (item 24)
6. Respond to all existing reviews (items 39-40)
7. Start weekly Google Posts (item 32)
8. Pre-populate Q&A with 5-10 common questions (item 41)
